The kitty catty :3

He doesn't quite understand that he is smaller than us, and often walks in front of us and we end up stepping on him. He likes to sit on and in cardboard boxes. He wishes death upon every human being for trapping him in this form. He likes cuddles.


He is the bestest most catest cat to ever cat!!!
